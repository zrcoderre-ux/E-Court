Attorney Fee
—
Rule 3.214(a) schedule

LASC Local Rule 3.214(a), “Contract Provision or Note” (new & effective July 1, 2011). Applies when a promissory note, contract, or statute provides for reasonable attorney fees. Computed on the amount of the judgment unless the court determines otherwise; a greater fee for extraordinary services requires an itemized application (Rule 3.214(d)). This is the schedule figure only — not legal advice.
